Intervau.AI is a modern AI-powered interview preparation and talent evaluation platform designed for both candidates and HR teams. This frontend project includes the complete user interface, navigation system, dashboards, interview screens, and supporting pages such as Resources, Testimonials, and About Us.

Interview history & performance analytics
